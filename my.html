If machine learning is a subfield of artificial intelligence, then deep learning could be called a subfield of machine learning. The evolution of the subject has gone artificial intelligence > machine learning > deep learning. The expression “deep learning” was first used when talking about Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs) by Igor Aizenberg and colleagues in or around 2000. Since then, the term has really started to take over the AI conversation, despite the fact that there are other branches of study taking place, like natural language processing, or NLP
